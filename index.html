Welcome to saltyrtc.org!

SaltyRTC is an end-to-end encrypted signalling protocol. It offers to freely choose from a range of signalling tasks, such as setting up a WebRTC or ORTC peer-to-peer connection. SaltyRTC is completely open to new and custom signalling tasks for everything feasible.
